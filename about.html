About Krishna Interior House

Krishna Interior House is a Lucknow-based interior solutions company founded and managed by Vijay Kumar Yadav. With years of hands-on experience in the interior industry, we have successfully completed hundreds of residential and commercial projects across Lucknow and neighboring areas.

Our mission is simple: deliver world-class interior work at honest prices, with every project completed on time and to the highest standards.

Since 2019 • Crafting Luxury

Our Story

What began as a passion for beautiful spaces has grown into one of Lucknow's most reliable and premium interior work companies.

Vijay Kumar Yadav started Krishna Interior House with a singular vision: to make premium, world-class interior solutions accessible to every homeowner and business in Lucknow. We never compromise on craftsmanship, timelines, or client satisfaction.
