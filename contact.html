Frequently Asked Questions

Find Answers to Common Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project duration varies based on the scope and complexity. A typical residential project takes 2-4 weeks. We always provide a detailed timeline during the consultation phase.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer completely free consultations where we understand your needs, vision, and budget to provide the best solutions.

What areas do you service?

We serve Lucknow and nearby neighborhoods including Gomti Nagar, Hazratganj, Aliganj, Indira Nagar, Faizabad Road, and Chinhat. Call or WhatsApp us to discuss your exact location.

What payment options do you offer?

We accept multiple payment options including cash, bank transfer, credit cards, and online payments. Payment terms will be discussed during the consultation.

Do you provide warranty on your work?

Yes, we provide warranty on all our work and materials. Warranty details will be provided in your project agreement.

Can I modify my design after starting the project?

Yes, modifications can be made during the project. However, changes may affect the timeline and budget. We discuss this during the initial consultation.
